In javascript, when we want to encode, we may use encodeURI and encodeURIComponent. What is the difference between them?

characters they do not encode

encodeURI will not encode the following 82 characters: !, #, $, &, ', (, ), *, +, ,, -, ., /, :, ;, =, ?, @, _, ~, 0-9, a-z, A-Z
